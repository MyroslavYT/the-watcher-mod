REPLACE THESE .OGG FILES WITH YOUR OWN AUDIO

Minecraft only plays .ogg format audio files.
Use convert_audio.bat (Windows) or convert_audio.sh (Mac/Linux) to convert MP3/MP4/WAV.

Files to replace:
  ambient_scream.ogg      - screaming heard during red world event
  breathing.ogg           - faint breathing when player stands still
  footsteps_echo.ogg      - echo of player's own footsteps
  jumpscare_sting.ogg     - loud sting at jumpscare moment
  world_red_ambience.ogg  - ambient sound when world goes red
  disc_distorted.ogg      - plays in jukebox (distorted music disc)

After replacing, rebuild the mod with: gradlew build
